## Problem: Low Frequency Data To High Frequency Estimated Data

Time is a (or the most) critical dimension in engineering or financial disciplines. Data collection processes often provide one with low frequency aggregated data that needs to be transformed to esimtated higher frequency data. Such transform is required in finance, engineering and data compression algorithms. 

## Spectral Technologies develops FDTT

Spectral Technologies has developed this transform. The transform that has been developed is called Fast Disaggregation in Time Technology (FDTT). FDTT is derived using variational principles and energy minimization techniques. 

## Applications

## Finance

Representation of quantities in time is often a critical task in finance. It is often done poorly by banks and energy firms. FDTT can help banks and energy firms represent these quantities in time more accurately.

## Discounted Cash Flow

FDTT has been applied to improve the Discounted Cash Flow, a core formula in equities markets. Such improved Discounted Cash Flow has been endorsed by Christopher Jones, Oxford PhD, Jump Trading Researcher. Furthermore, FDTT has also been applied to EIA Natural Gas Market Data, and is in usage at major, global hedge fund for their commodities division.

## Data Compression Algorithms

Continuous data is common in many applications. To represent this data in its entirety, an infinite number of bits is required. Compression of this continuous signal can be done with FDTT reducing the information required for transmission and storage.
